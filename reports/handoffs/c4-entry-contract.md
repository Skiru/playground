# C4 Entry Contract

## Prerequisites for Checkpoint C4 (Places Media and Uploads)
- **C3 Completion State:** All C3 features (redesign, Google Identity, Favorites, Visits) must be 100% completed, verified, and passing in both unit and integration suites.
- **Media Scope:** C4 will exclusively focus on:
  - Place images and galleries.
  - S3/local media storage.
  - Administration image uploads.
  - Optimizing card rendering with visual thumbnails.
- **Out of Scope for C4:** Reviews, comments, forum, and server deployment remain strictly out of scope to avoid broadening.
